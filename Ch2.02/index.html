Implicit Differentiation is the strategy we are going to use to derive equations of this type. The rules are simple.

General Process

  1. Derive each "x" and "y" using our standard derivation rules.
  2. Add a to the end of each "y" after it has been derived.

Derive x3y2 + y3=3

  1. x3y2 is "product rule" and derives to 3x2y2 + x32y
    y3 is a monomial and derives to 3y2
    3 is a constant and derives to 0.

    3x2y2 + x32y + 3y2 = 0 is our solution
  2. Notice that we have to add to the second and third term because y was derived in each of those terms.
